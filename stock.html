If you buy a stock at Rs 120 and then later again at Rs 150, how will you calculate your net returns when you sell the shares acquired cumulatively?

The Stock Average Calculator allows you to do just that.

To calculate the average share price:

  • Enter the price of the first purchase along with quantity followed by all subsequent purchases.
  • Click on the Calculate Average button

Example of Stock Average Calculator

Consider you have made two purchases of a stock: 100 shares at a price of Rs 250 and 200 shares at a price of Rs 275.

We will apply the formula for calculating the average price, which is:

((A2*B2)+(A3*B3) + (An*Bn))/(A2+A3+An), where n is the number of your last purchase.

Since we have made two purchases in the above example, we will consider:

((100 x 250)+(200*275))/(100+200)=266.66
